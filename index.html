About Me

Jolinda Mae Silva

As a Front-End Developer, my role is to create
the user-facing portion of websites and web
applications. This includes everything that the
user sees and interacts with on the website or
application, such as the layout, design, and
functionality.

I love being a Front-End Developer because it
allows me to be creative while also using my
technical skills to build something that people
will use and enjoy. It's a challenging and
rewarding career that requires constant learning
and growth, but it's also incredibly fulfilling
to see something I created come to life on the web.
